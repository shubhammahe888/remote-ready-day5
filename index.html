‚úÖ The Winning Strategy: Quality + Tracking + Follow-Up

The 80/20 Rule

80% of your time: Networking, building relationships at target companies, getting on recruiter radars, LinkedIn presence.

20% of your time: Strategic applications to roles where you have a connection or can get a warm intro.

Why this works: 85% of jobs are filled through connections, yet people spend 100% of time cold applying.

The 5-10-20 Framework

5 "Dream" applications per week: 2-3 hours each, full customization, warm intro if possible. Expected: 20-30% response rate.

10 "Target" applications per week: 1 hour each, tailored resume + cover letter. Expected: 10-15% response rate.

20 "Backup" applications per month: 15-20 min each, resume optimization only. Expected: 2-5% response rate.

Result: ~11 interviews per month, offer in 8-10 weeks.

Track Everything

You can't optimize what you don't measure. Log every application, response, interview. Analyze what's working and double down on it.

Job seekers who track: Land jobs 20-30% faster than those who don't.

Follow Up Consistently

Day 7: First follow-up email. Day 14: Second follow-up. Most people don't do this‚Äîthat's your advantage.

Data: Follow-ups double your response rate (10% ‚Üí 20%).
